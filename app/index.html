about us

Hungry People is a Turkish cuisine restaurant. Only here you can taste authentic masterpieces from our Turkish masters. Dishes are prepared right in front of your eyes - in a wood oven, tandoor and grill. We will be glad to see you visiting us!

The Hungry People cafe menu combines both traditional Turkish and European, Mediterranean dishes. The main feature of each dish is that each slice of fresh bread, juicy meat is prepared by skilled chefs, whom we invited directly from Turkey.

OUR TEAM

MASTER CHEF

Chef - Ibrahim Kaya, from his great-great-grandfather a chef from the city of Izmir, one of the best chefs in Turkey.

Ibrahim has gone from a simple chef to a high-class professional, he has undergone training and internships in the best restaurants in Italy and Germany and is now involved in the most responsible projects.
